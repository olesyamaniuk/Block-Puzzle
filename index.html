Game Description

Block Puzzle - Shift is a fun and relaxing puzzle game that puts your logic skills to the test! The goal is simple – shift blocks around to complete full rows and columns, clearing space for new pieces. With an easy-to-learn but hard-to-master mechanic, this game offers endless entertainment for players of all ages. Whether you're looking for a quick brain workout or a way to unwind, Block Puzzle - Shift is the perfect game for you!

Features

  • Engaging Gameplay – A fun and addictive puzzle experience.

  • Simple Controls – Easy-to-learn mechanics suitable for all ages.

  • Multiple Levels – Challenge yourself with various difficulty settings.

  • Relaxing Soundtrack – Enjoy a calming puzzle-solving experience.

  • Offline Play – No internet connection required!

How to Play

Drag and shift blocks to fit them into the grid.

Plan your moves carefully to avoid running out of space.

Challenge yourself with different levels and increasing difficulty!
